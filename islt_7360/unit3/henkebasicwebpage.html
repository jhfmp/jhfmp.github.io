Mr. Henke's Basic Webpage

This is my first attempt at creating a webpage.
The following are two possibilities for my final website:

Topic 1

A Webpage for Students and Parents

My first idea is to create a webpage where parents and students could access important information and resources. Ideas to include in a classroom webpage:

  • newsletters
  • calendars
  • photo gallery
  • vocabulary lists
  • literacy links

Topic 2

A Webpage to Share Strategies with Other Special Education Teachers

My second idea is to create a webpage where special education teachers could share information about strategies they implement in their classrooms. Ideas, techniques, critiques, and materials could be shared. Examples include:

  • visuals
  • token boards
  • reading resources
  • communication sheets
  • strategy reviews
